Recipe of Today: Honeydew Melon

  • 3 cups Honeydew Melon (seeded & chopped)
  • 2 tsp Lime Juice
  • 1 cup Vanilla Yogurt
  • 1 cup Ice Cubes
  • Optional: 1 tbl Honey or Sugar
  1. Add honeydew melon cubes to blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Add yogurt, ice and lime juice and blend it again.
  3. Sample your smoothie and add honey or sugar if needed. Blend again.
  4. Transfer into tall glasses and garnish with melon slice.
Preparation time
10 Minutes
Number of servings (12 oz)
2
Calories per serving
250
295 if 1 tbs sugar added
315 if 1 tbs honey added
